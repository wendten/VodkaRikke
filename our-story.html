The Brand

Born in Chaos, Built in Gold

It didn’t start in a boardroom. It started at a festival — mud underfoot, music in the air, and a line of people waiting for toilets no one wanted to step into. Ugly, broken, unforgettable for all the wrong reasons.

That moment sparked an idea: take the worst experience and turn it into something unforgettable for the right reasons. What began as a raw, unfiltered need grew into a vision — transforming the lowest standard into something bold, excessive, and impossible to ignore. From plastic and stench… to gold, to diamonds, to pure statement.

At the same time, cheap liquor was part of the same story — passed around, thoughtless, disposable. That changed too. What started as necessity became obsession, refinement, and ultimately one of the finest vodka brands in the world. A brand built on contrast: from the dirtiest festival toilets to bathrooms made of gold and diamonds, from the cheapest liquor on the ground to uncompromising premium quality.
